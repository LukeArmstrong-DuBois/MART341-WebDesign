Beginner Yoga Poses

Here are some poses that I did while starting my yoga journey.
Master these three poses, and you'll be ready for more advanced ones.


Child's Pose Hold 90 sec Forward Fold Hold 45 sec Low Lunge Hold 20 sec

While these poses might seem easy, they will be mainstays in your yoga practices over the years!
Holding poses like these for a few moments a day can be more beneficial to your body than complex ones that are popular on social media.

Here is a checklist for you to keep track of what poses you've completed:

Child's Pose
Forward Fold
Low Lunge
