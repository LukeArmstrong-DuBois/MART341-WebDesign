The Delicious Food of Winter

Everyone has their own favorite food to eat when it's cold out, but here are some suggestions.

Spicy soup to warm your guts.
Hearty stew With either meat or vegetables.
Rich hot chocolate to sip on when cold.
Make an army of cookie people.
Roasted chicken is good for leftovers.
Flourless chocolate cake is a family favorite.
