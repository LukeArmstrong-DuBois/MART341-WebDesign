Why I Chose These Website Ideas

Art Portfolio

I chose this idea because it fits with my passions. I have many pieces of art that have never been uploaded to the Internet.
This portfolio would be a hub for all my future works and allow friends and family to see my art.

Pet Blog

I chose this idea because I live with seven pets, and they are always doing exciting things.
I also really like writing, so a blog about something significant to me (my animals) makes sense.

Yoga Tutorials

I chose this idea because I have been practicing yoga for a long time and have experience teaching classes.
Making short yoga routines accessible on the website would be an interesting project, so this idea is my favorite out of the three I mentioned
