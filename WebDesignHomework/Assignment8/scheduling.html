Why make a schedule?

Yoga can be a difficult practice to maintain.
To help with this issue, I recommend making a schedule to hold yourself accountable for your practice.
Even if it's only for a few minutes a day, incorporating yoga into your daily life can give you the opportunity to relax when you're having a stressful day.

My recommendation would be to buy a journal and write down what times work with your schedule.
Then, find some poses at your skill level and combine them in a 15 to 20-minute practice.
