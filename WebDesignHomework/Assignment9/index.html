Meditation in 6 Easy Steps

Step 1: Find a Good Location

Environment matters a lot when meditating, so make sure you find a quiet spot to practice.

Step 2: Relax Your Body

When seated, try to loosen your body so it feels heavy. Any tension in your body can draw your action away from relaxing.

Step 3: Focus on Breathing

Closing your eyes and focusing on your breath can help you move from your mind into your body.

Step 4: Move Past Distractions

You're bound to have things pop into your mind; don't get discouraged when this happens. Return to focusing on your breathing.

Step 5: Mindfulness in Present Moment

When you're truly relaxed, you should begin noticing sensations, sounds, and feelings you've taken for granted throughout the day.

Step 6: Return When it Feels Right

When ready, slowly move your body to exit the practice. You should now be more relaxed and prepared to continue your day.
