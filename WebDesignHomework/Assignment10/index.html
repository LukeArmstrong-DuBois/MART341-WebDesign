The Art of Pumpkin Carving

Pumpkin carving is a fun way to celebrate an excellent holiday. Turning a simple pumpkin into a glowing work of art has been a tradition of the fall season since ancient times.
Here are some tips to make your pumpkin carving go smoothly!

Tool Suggestions

To get started, you will need to get some equipment:

  • Carving knife
  • Scooping tool
  • Bucket for Seeds and "guts"
  • Marker (optional)
  • Stencils (optional)

Make The Design Choice

After you grab your tools, you can start carving!

Scary or silly, it's your choice!

Enjoy your creation!

When you're done with your pumpkin, put a light source inside it and turn off the lights. Enjoy!
